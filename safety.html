THE DANGERS OF SCAM LINKS

Youtube, Instagram, & Discord

how can i stay safe on the internet?

1. recognize what a phishing scam looks like so you can avoid clicking on it.

2. use security software to protect yourself against scams.

3. use multi-factor authentication.

4. back up your data.

5. do not click on suspicious links! (even your close friends can send these!!)

6. use a url scanner to check if a link is safe or not (ex: virustotal, scanurl, URL void).

7. never give out personal information.

8. keep up to date with your software.
